About OMIToolKit

Free tools that respect your time and your data.

OMIToolKit is a growing collection of everyday utility tools — QR codes, passwords, PDFs, images and more — built to run entirely inside your browser.

Why no sign-up?

Most tool websites ask for an account, then quietly limit what you can do for free. OMIToolKit doesn't store any accounts, so there's nothing to limit. Every tool is free, unlimited, and ready the moment the page loads.

Why no uploads?

Every tool here is written in plain JavaScript and runs locally on your device. Your files, your passwords, your data — none of it is ever sent to a server, because none of these tools have one.

More tools, every week

New tools are added regularly across utility, image, PDF, video and audio categories. If there's a tool you'd like to see, it's likely already on the roadmap.
