Extracting video thumbnails

Video thumbnail extraction works by seeking the video to a specific timestamp and drawing the current frame onto an HTML5 Canvas element, then converting that canvas image to a PNG file for download. This is the same technique used by video editing software. Use the "Capture 6 frames" button to automatically extract evenly-spaced frames across the entire video for a quick visual overview.
