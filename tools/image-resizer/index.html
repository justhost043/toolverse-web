How to resize an image without losing quality

The key is to use high-quality downscaling (enabled by default here with imageSmoothingQuality: "high"). When resizing to a much smaller size, a two-pass Lanczos-style approach works best — scale to 50% first, then scale again to the target. For upscaling beyond 200%, quality will always decrease since pixels must be invented. The WebP format typically produces 25–35% smaller files than JPEG at equal quality.

What are the correct social media image sizes?

Instagram feed posts: 1080×1080px (square) or 1080×1350px (portrait). Instagram/Facebook Stories: 1080×1920px. Facebook cover: 820×312px. Twitter/X header: 1500×500px. LinkedIn banner: 1584×396px. YouTube thumbnail: 1280×720px. Pinterest pin: 1000×1500px. Use the Presets tab to select these sizes instantly.
