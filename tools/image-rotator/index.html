When do you need to rotate an image?

Photos taken on mobile devices sometimes save with incorrect orientation — they appear sideways or upside-down when viewed on a computer. Rotating corrects this. Flipping (mirroring) is useful for creating symmetrical designs, correcting selfie mirror effects, or creating left/right variants of a graphic.
