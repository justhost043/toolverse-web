What are fade in and fade out effects?

A fade in gradually increases the volume from silence to full at the start of a track. A fade out gradually decreases volume to silence at the end. These effects are standard in music production, podcast editing, and video soundtracks — they prevent jarring starts and abrupt endings that feel unprofessional.
