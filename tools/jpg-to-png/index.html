Why convert JPG to PNG?

PNG supports transparency (alpha channel) while JPG does not. If you need a transparent background, logos, icons, or images with sharp edges on solid colours, PNG is the better format. PNG is also lossless — converting from JPG to PNG will not reduce quality further, though it won't recover quality already lost in the original JPG compression.
