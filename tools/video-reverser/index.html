Creative uses for reversed video

Reversed video is a popular creative effect — reversed water splashes, reversed explosions, reversed dance moves, and reversed speech all create compelling visuals. It is also used in music videos, viral content, and movie special effects. The reverse filter in FFmpeg reads all frames into memory before outputting, which is why large files take longer to process.
