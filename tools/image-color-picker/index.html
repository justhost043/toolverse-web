How to use the image color picker

Drop or upload any image, then move your mouse over the image — a magnified preview follows your cursor for pixel-precise selection. Click anywhere to "lock in" the color at that point. The HEX, RGB and HSL values appear below the image, and each can be copied with a single click. Your pick history is shown as color swatches — click any previous pick to copy it again.

Why pick colors from images?

Designers frequently need to match colors from photos, brand assets, screenshots or reference images. Instead of estimating visually, an image color picker gives you the exact hex code of any pixel. This is useful when building websites to match a client's brand photo, when designing marketing materials to stay consistent with existing imagery, or when recreating a color scheme you've seen in another design.
