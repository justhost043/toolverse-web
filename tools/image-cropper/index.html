How to crop an image

Drop or select your image. Then click and drag on the image to draw your crop selection — a darkened overlay shows the area that will be removed. Choose a preset aspect ratio to lock the selection to a specific proportion, or leave it on "Free" to draw any rectangle. Click "Crop & Download" to save the cropped image at its original resolution.

Aspect ratio presets explained

1:1 is a square — perfect for Instagram posts and profile pictures. 16:9 is widescreen — ideal for YouTube thumbnails, presentations and desktop wallpapers. 4:3 is the classic photo format. 9:16 is vertical — for Instagram Stories, TikTok and Reels. 2:3 portrait is the standard photo print ratio (4×6 inch prints).
