How to merge PDF files online

Upload your PDFs by dropping them above or clicking to select. Drag the handles to reorder files, then click Merge PDFs. Your combined file downloads instantly. Everything runs in your browser — files are never uploaded to any server.

Is it safe to merge PDFs here?

This tool uses pdf-lib, an open-source JavaScript library that processes files entirely on your device. Confidential documents, medical records, and financial files stay completely private.
