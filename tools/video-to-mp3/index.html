How video to MP3 conversion works

Your video file contains separate video and audio streams. FFmpeg strips the video stream and re-encodes or copies the audio stream to your chosen format. MP3 is the most universally supported audio format — every device and app can play it. WAV preserves 100% of the original audio quality. OGG and AAC offer good quality at smaller file sizes for web use.
