Recording webcam and screen video

This tool uses the browser MediaRecorder API to capture video from your camera or screen directly. Camera recording captures both video and audio from your webcam and microphone. Screen recording captures everything on your screen — useful for tutorials, demos, and bug reports. All recordings are saved locally in WebM format, which is supported by all modern browsers and can be played in VLC on desktop.
