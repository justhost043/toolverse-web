How to use speech to text

Click the microphone button and grant microphone permission when the browser asks. Start speaking — your words will appear in real time. The grey italic text shows what the recognizer is currently processing; it turns to solid text when confirmed. Enable "Continuous mode" to keep listening after each pause; otherwise, recognition stops after a brief silence.

Browser support

This tool uses the Web Speech API, which works best in Google Chrome and Microsoft Edge on desktop. It is not supported in Firefox or Safari. On mobile, Chrome for Android supports speech recognition. The quality of transcription depends on your microphone quality and the ambient noise level in your environment.
