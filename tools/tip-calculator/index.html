How much should you tip?

Tipping norms vary by country and service type. In the United States, 15–20% is standard for restaurant service, with 20%+ for exceptional service. For delivery drivers, $3–5 per order is common. For hotel housekeeping, $2–5 per night. For hair stylists and barbers, 15–20% of the service cost. In many countries outside North America, tipping is optional or not expected at all — the price you see is the price you pay.
