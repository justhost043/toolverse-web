What is WCAG contrast ratio?

WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) defines minimum contrast ratios to ensure text is readable for people with visual impairments. AA level requires a ratio of at least 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text (18pt / 24px or 14pt bold / 18.67px bold). AAA level requires 7:1 for normal text and 4.5:1 for large text. A ratio of 21:1 (black on white) is the maximum possible contrast.
