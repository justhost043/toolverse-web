How to use the time zone converter

Select the time zone you're converting from, the time zone you're converting to, then enter a date and time. The converted result updates automatically. You can also click any city in the world clocks section to instantly use that city's time zone as the "From" field.

Understanding UTC offsets

UTC (Coordinated Universal Time) is the global standard for time. Every time zone is expressed as UTC plus or minus a number of hours. Pakistan (PKT) is UTC+5, London (GMT/BST) is UTC+0 in winter and UTC+1 in summer, New York is UTC-5 in winter and UTC-4 in summer. The converter handles daylight saving time automatically based on the selected date.
