Uses for flipping video

Horizontal flip (mirror) is useful for selfie camera videos that appear reversed, creating symmetrical visual effects, or correcting footage from action cameras mounted facing the wrong direction. Vertical flip fixes upside-down footage from cameras mounted inverted. Both operations are applied directly to the video frames with no quality loss.
