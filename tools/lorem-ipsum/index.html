What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is dummy placeholder text used by designers, developers and printers since the 1500s. It's derived from "de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum" by Cicero, written in 45 BC — but the text has been scrambled and altered so that readers don't focus on the words themselves, only on the visual layout around them.

Why use placeholder text?

When presenting a design, using real content too early can distract reviewers into critiquing the writing rather than the layout, typography, and spacing. Lorem Ipsum keeps attention on the visual structure. Developers use it to test how a UI component handles different text lengths before real content is available.

How many paragraphs do I need?

For a standard blog post layout, 3–5 paragraphs give a realistic sense of how the page will look. For email templates, 1–2 paragraphs are usually enough. For a full landing page or long-form article mock-up, use 8–12 paragraphs to test how the page scrolls and how headers break up the content.

Can I use Lorem Ipsum in a real published page?

No — it should always be replaced with real content before publishing. Search engines may flag pages with Lorem Ipsum text as thin or low-quality content.

Is Lorem Ipsum Latin?

It's based on Latin but is not grammatically correct. Several words have been altered or invented specifically to make the passage semantically meaningless — so readers see letter patterns without reading real words.
