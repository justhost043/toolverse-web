Creative uses for reversed audio

Reversed audio is used in music production for reverse reverb effects, creative intros, and psychoacoustic tricks. Reversing speech is also used in audio forensics and privacy protection. Some musicians record a melody reversed and then flip it to create unique melodic phrases that have a natural "backward feel" when played forward.
