Which QR code type should I use?

URL opens a website when scanned — most common use case. WiFi connects guests to your network without typing a password — perfect for cafes, hotels, and offices. vCard adds your contact details to someone's phone with a single scan. Email pre-fills an email draft. Phone dials a number. Text displays any message.

What size QR code should I print?

For business cards, 200-300px at actual print size (around 2cm × 2cm) is sufficient. For posters, use 500px or above — the larger the print, the further away it can be scanned. As a rule: the scanning distance should be 10× the QR code's physical size or less.
