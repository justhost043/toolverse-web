When to watermark your videos

Watermarking protects your creative work from unauthorized use and promotes your brand when videos are shared. For YouTube creators, adding your channel name at the bottom keeps your branding visible when clips are shared on social media. For businesses, CONFIDENTIAL or DRAFT watermarks prevent distribution of internal review copies. The semi-transparent text style ensures watermarks are visible without obstructing the content.
