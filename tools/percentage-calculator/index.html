Which percentage calculator do I need?

Use "% of a number" when you have a percentage and a total and want to find the actual amount — for example, 20% off a $500 item. Use "% change" when you want to know how much something increased or decreased in percentage terms — stock prices, sales figures, or weight loss. Use "X is what % of Y" when you have two numbers and want to express one as a percentage of the other — for example, a test score of 75 out of 90.
