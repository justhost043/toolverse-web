How to record from your microphone

Click the red microphone button to start recording — your browser will ask permission to access your microphone. Click again to stop. Each recording appears below with playback controls and a download button. Recordings are processed entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.

Which format should I choose?

WebM is the default and works in Chrome, Edge, and Firefox. WAV produces uncompressed audio — the highest quality but larger file size. OGG is an open-source format with good compression. For most uses, WebM is the best choice. If you need the recording for professional editing software, use WAV.
