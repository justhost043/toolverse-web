Video speed and audio pitch

Changing video speed with this tool adjusts both the video frame rate and audio playback speed using the atempo filter for audio. FFmpeg's atempo filter supports 0.5x to 2x in a single pass. For speeds outside this range, the tool chains multiple atempo filters. Audio below 0.5x may sound unnatural. For cinematic slow motion, consider recording at a higher frame rate (60fps or 120fps) and playing at 24fps instead.
