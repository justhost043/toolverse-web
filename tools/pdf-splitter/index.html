How to split a PDF

Choose your split mode: Split all pages extracts every page into a separate PDF file (downloaded as a ZIP). Page range extracts a consecutive range of pages into one PDF. Pick pages lets you select individual non-consecutive pages to extract into one PDF. All processing happens in your browser — your file is never uploaded to any server.
