How to use the unit converter

Select a category (Length, Weight, Temperature, etc.), choose your "From" and "To" units, type a value, and the converted result appears instantly. Use the swap button to reverse the conversion direction without reselecting the units.

Temperature conversions explained

Unlike most unit types, temperature conversions aren't simple multiplications — they involve offset adjustments. Celsius to Fahrenheit, for example, uses the formula (°C × 9/5) + 32. This converter handles all the formulas internally, so you can just enter the number and get the answer.
