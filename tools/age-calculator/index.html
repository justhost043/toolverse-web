How to calculate your exact age

Enter your date of birth and the date you want to calculate your age as of (it defaults to today). The calculator gives your age broken down into years, months, days and weeks, plus total days and approximate hours lived. It also shows how many days remain until your next birthday.

Why might my age in months seem high?

The months count shows your total age expressed in complete months — not just the months beyond your last birthday. For example, someone who is 25 years and 3 months old would show 303 months. This is useful for medical or legal contexts where exact age in months is required.
