When to use mute video

Common reasons to remove audio: replacing background music with a copyright-free track, silencing a video before adding a professional voiceover, creating clean footage for a video essay, removing accidental audio from a screen recording, or sharing a clip that contains sensitive conversations. The video quality is completely preserved — only the audio stream is removed.
