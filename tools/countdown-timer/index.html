Uses for a countdown timer

Countdown timers are useful for tracking time remaining to product launches, exams, vacations, project deadlines, holidays, birthdays, weddings, or any significant date. By saving multiple countdowns, you can keep an eye on several upcoming events at once without losing track of any.
