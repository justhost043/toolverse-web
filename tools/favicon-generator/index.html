What favicon sizes do I need?

Modern websites need multiple favicon sizes to look sharp across all browsers and devices. The classic 16×16 and 32×32 are used in browser tabs and bookmarks. 180×180 is the Apple Touch Icon for iOS home screens. 192×192 and 512×512 are used by Android and Progressive Web Apps (PWA). 48×48 is used by some Windows browsers. This generator creates all of them in one click.

Why should my favicon be square?

Browsers and operating systems display favicons in square or circular crops. Starting with a square image ensures nothing important gets cropped. If your logo is wider than it is tall (landscape), consider creating a square version that contains just the icon or symbol part of your logo for the best favicon result.
