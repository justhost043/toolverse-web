Why convert SVG to PNG?

SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) is ideal for icons and logos that need to scale without losing quality, but many platforms — social media, certain CMS systems, older email clients — don't support SVG and require raster formats like PNG or JPG. Converting gives you a fixed-resolution image while keeping full control over the output size.
