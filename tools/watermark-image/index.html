Why watermark your images?

Watermarking protects your creative work from being used without credit. Adding your brand name, website URL, or logo discourages unauthorized use and increases brand visibility when images are shared on social media. For photographers and digital artists, watermarking is an essential step before sharing preview images of paid work.
