Fast cut vs re-encoded cut

The "fast cut" option copies the video stream without re-encoding — this is instant and lossless but the cut points may be slightly inaccurate (up to a few seconds) because it can only cut at keyframes. Unchecking fast cut re-encodes the video for frame-accurate cuts but takes longer. For most uses — cutting out a middle section or extracting a highlight — fast cut is the better choice.
