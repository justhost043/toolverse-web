When to rotate videos

Videos recorded in portrait mode on a phone often appear sideways when played on a computer. This happens because the phone stores rotation metadata that some players ignore. This tool permanently rotates the video frames — not just the metadata — so it displays correctly everywhere including social media platforms, video editors, and TVs.
