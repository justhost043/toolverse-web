Why convert GIF to MP4?

GIF files are much larger than equivalent MP4 videos — sometimes 10-20x larger. A 5MB GIF can become a 200KB MP4 with the same visual quality. For websites and social media, MP4 videos load much faster and use less bandwidth. Twitter, Discord, Slack, and most modern platforms automatically convert GIFs to MP4 behind the scenes for this exact reason.
