Video format compatibility

MP4 with H.264 codec is the most universally compatible video format — it plays on every device, browser, smart TV, and platform. WebM is optimized for web browsers and produces smaller files. MKV is a container that can hold any codec and is popular for high-quality archiving. MOV is Apple's native format, best for Final Cut Pro workflows.
