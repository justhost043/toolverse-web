Trim vs Cut

Trimming removes from the start or end of a video — like trimming the fat from a steak. Cutting extracts a specific middle section. Use this tool to quickly remove a countdown intro, an accidental recording at the end, or skip a slow opening. The fast copy mode preserves full video quality with no re-encoding.
