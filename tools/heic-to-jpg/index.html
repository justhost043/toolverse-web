What is HEIC format?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is the default photo format on iPhones since iOS 11. It offers better compression than JPG — smaller file sizes at equal or better quality. However, Windows PCs, older Android devices, and many apps and websites don't support HEIC. Converting to JPG ensures your photos open everywhere without any special software.
