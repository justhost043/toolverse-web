How audio cutting works

Audio cutting extracts a specific time range from your audio file. The start and end times are converted to sample positions (multiplied by the sample rate), and only those samples are kept in the output file. This is a lossless operation — no re-compression happens within the selected range, so audio quality is preserved perfectly.
