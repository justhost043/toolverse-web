How the volume booster works

This tool uses the Web Audio API to decode your audio file, applies a gain (multiplication) to the audio signal, then re-encodes it for download. A gain of 2x means every sample value is doubled — the audio will sound twice as loud. Values above 4x may cause clipping (distortion) if the original audio is already loud. For best results, start with 2x and increase gradually.

Why does audio need volume boosting?

Videos recorded on phones in noisy environments, podcast recordings from budget microphones, and audio extracted from screen recordings often come out too quiet. Rather than editing in a DAW, this quick tool lets you boost the volume in seconds and download the result.
